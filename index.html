Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

We collect the following personal data:

  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • Identity document (Carte Nationale d’Identité)

Purpose of Data Collection

Your personal data is collected and used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Name and date of birth are used to uniquely identify users.
  • Identity document (CNI) is used to verify that the user is legally an adult.
  • Identity documents may also be used to train and improve our internal model for automatic date-of-birth extraction and age verification. This processing is internal and not shared with third parties.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third parties.

All personal data is stored securely on our own infrastructure using Firebase servers.

Data Retention

Your personal data is retained only as long as necessary.

  • Users may request deletion of their data at any time by sending us a message.
  • All stored personal data will be permanently deleted no later than December 31, 2026.
  • After this date, no personal data will be stored or retained.

Delete Your Personal Data

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data at any time.

To do so, simply contact us. Once requested, your data will be scheduled for deletion and permanently removed at the latest by December 31, 2026.
